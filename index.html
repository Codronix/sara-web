Mission & Vission Lend a Paw

Sorsogon Animal
Rescue and Adoption

"From lost and homeless to loved and cherished.
From abandoned to adored."



Mission

To protect animals ,promote their well-being, and help them find their forever home. Preventing cruelty, promoting compassion to animals, advocating for pet related issues and to educate the public about responsible pet ownership and animal welfare law.



Vision

To achieve through efforts the reduction of numbers of stray dogs and cats, cruelty and neglected. Also, to prevent rabies free and promote responsible pet owners
